What is Monster's Fling?

Monster's Fling is for lonely souls who want to find their stranger half. Our site wants to show that everybody deserves love, no matter if you have horns, scales, fur or pointy teeth.

Let's start matching

What are you waiting for? Let's find some matches! Click the bottom below to start. You are going to see a picture and a description of different monsters. If you like someone, click the heart symbol.

If not, continue to the next monster by pressing the right arrow. You can Always go back and like someone you skipped earlier.
